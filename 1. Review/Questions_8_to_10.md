## Q8: Electricity vs. Electronics
**Definition:**
* **Electricity** refers to the physical flow of electric charge and energy. It is primarily concerned with the **generation, transmission, and powering** of loads (e.g., motors, heaters, lights).
* **Electronics** is the science of **controlling** electron flow (using semiconductors like transistors) to process **information** and signals.

**Key Difference:**
> Electricity is about energy volume; Electronics is about signal precision and logic.

## Q9: Electronic Device Analysis
**Device:** Laptop Computer

**Role of Electronics:**
A laptop relies entirely on electronics to function.
* **CPU:** Uses billions of transistors as microscopic switches to process binary data (0s and 1s).
* **Display:** Electronic drivers control individual pixels to form images.
* **Communication:** Wi-Fi modules manipulate low-power signals to transmit data wirelessly.

**Conclusion:** Without electronic components to control the current, a laptop would just be a battery with no computing ability.
