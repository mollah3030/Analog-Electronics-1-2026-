# Reflection on Diode Applications

## Key Learning Outcomes

During this assignment, I gained a deeper understanding of how to determine thermal voltage through the formula $V_T = \frac{kT}{q}$. I also explored the exponential relationship of diode current using the Shockley equation. Furthermore, I developed practical skills in circuit analysis by comparing and applying both the ideal and approximate diode models to various problems.

## Primary Challenges

The most significant difficulty I faced was accurately identifying the state of the diode (whether it is in the **ON** or **OFF** position) based on the circuit's polarity. Additionally, ensuring that Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law (KVL) and Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL) were applied correctly while accounting for the specific voltage drop of the diode required careful attention to detail.
